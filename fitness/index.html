Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to download an app?

No. Trader Hawk works entirely through iMessage — the texting app already on your iPhone. No apps, no logins, no learning curve.

Can I share member transformation photos?

Yes! Send any photo via iMessage and the AI creates a professional transformation post. Just make sure you have your member's permission first.

Will it help me fill off-peak classes?

Absolutely. Text "make a promo for the 11am class" and the AI creates a targeted post to fill those empty spots. You can schedule recurring promotions for consistently low-attendance times.

Can multiple trainers use it?

Yes. Each trainer can have their own text thread with the AI. Great for multi-trainer gyms where everyone contributes content.

Can it post without my approval?

Never. Every post requires your explicit "go." You always see a preview first. Nothing goes live without your thumbs up.

How long does setup take?

2-3 business days. We connect your accounts, configure for your gym and class types, and do a 30-minute training call.

Does it integrate with my membership software?

We include your sign-up/trial link in every post. Direct integrations with platforms like Wodify, PushPress, and Mindbody are on our roadmap.

What happens if I cancel?

30-day notice. All your accounts stay yours. Credentials deleted within 7 days. No penalty. Setup fee is non-refundable.
