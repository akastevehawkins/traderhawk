The Challenge

Kimberly Warner is a full-time real estate agent serving Racine and Kenosha counties. Like most solo agents, she knew consistent social media was essential for generating leads — but the reality was brutal.

Between writing posts, finding stock images, scheduling content, managing her email, and trying to keep up with trends, Kimberly was spending 2+ hours every day on marketing tasks that pulled her away from what she does best: selling homes and serving clients.

She'd tried Canva for graphics, Hootsuite for scheduling, and even hired a part-time VA — but the tools were fragmented, the VA needed constant direction, and she still ended up doing most of the work herself. Some weeks, she'd fall behind entirely, and her pages would go silent.
