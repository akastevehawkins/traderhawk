Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to download an app?

No. Trader Hawk works entirely through iMessage — the texting app already on your iPhone. No apps, no logins, no learning curve.

What kind of business is this for?

Any local business that needs marketing but doesn't have the time or budget for a team. Restaurants, contractors, salons, gyms, retail, professional services — if you serve local customers, it works for you.

How is this different from Hootsuite or Buffer?

Those tools still need you to sit at a computer, write copy, design images, and schedule everything. Trader Hawk does all of that from a single text message. You describe what you want — it handles everything else.

Will it sound like me or like a robot?

Like you. The AI learns from every edit you make. After a few posts, it matches your voice, tone, and personality. Most users say people can't tell the difference.

Can it post without my approval?

Never. Every post requires your explicit "go." You always see a preview first. Nothing goes live without your thumbs up.

How long does setup take?

2-3 business days. We connect your accounts, configure the AI for your specific business, and do a 30-minute training call.

What if I'm not tech savvy?

Perfect. You text "post about our sale this weekend" and the AI does the rest. If you can text a friend, you can use Trader Hawk. We do all the technical setup for you.

What happens if I cancel?

30-day notice. All your accounts stay yours. Credentials deleted within 7 days. No penalty. Setup fee is non-refundable.
