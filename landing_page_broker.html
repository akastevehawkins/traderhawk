Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI learn my review standards?

During setup, you share your review checklist, preferred forms, common corrections, and any "rules of the house." We train the AI on all of it. Then every time you correct the AI or override a recommendation, it learns and adjusts. Within two weeks it's reviewing like you — within a month it's catching things you'd miss at midnight.

Do my agents need to install anything?

No. Everything works through iMessage. Your agents text contracts and questions to a dedicated number. No apps, no portals, no passwords. If they can text, they can use it.

Can the AI make decisions on my behalf?

Never. The AI flags issues, suggests corrections, and coaches agents — but it never approves, submits, or sends anything without a human in the loop. You set the rules, the AI enforces them. Final judgment is always yours.

What if an agent sends something the AI can't handle?

The AI knows its limits. If a contract has unusual terms, an unfamiliar addendum, or anything outside your training data, it escalates directly to you with a summary of what it found and what it's unsure about. You only see the hard stuff.

Is my agents' contract data secure?

Everything runs on dedicated hardware — not shared cloud infrastructure. Your agents' contracts are processed locally and never stored on third-party servers. We encrypt all data at rest and in transit. When you cancel, we wipe everything within 7 days.

How many agents can use it?

Standard supports up to 10 agents. Premium supports up to 50. If you have a larger team, contact us for custom pricing. Every agent texts the same number — no individual setup required.

What happens if I cancel?

30-day notice, and we deactivate the AI. All your training data and standards remain yours — we can export them. We delete all stored documents within 7 days. No penalty, no hard feelings.

Can it handle Wisconsin-specific forms?

Yes. We pre-train on all standard WB forms (WB-11, WB-4, WB-14, etc.) and Wisconsin-specific disclosure requirements. During your setup, we layer on your brokerage's specific preferences.
