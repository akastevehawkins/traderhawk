Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to download an app?

No. Trader Hawk works entirely through iMessage — the texting app already on your iPhone. No apps, no logins, no learning curve.

Can I send before/after photos from my phone?

Yes. Send any photo via iMessage and the AI will create a polished project showcase post with your caption, ready to publish to Facebook and Instagram.

Will it help me get more Google reviews?

Absolutely. After every job, text "closed deal" with the customer's name and the AI generates a personalized review request you can forward. It even sets a 14-day calendar reminder.

What if I do multiple trades?

No problem. The AI is configured for your specific services — whether that's plumbing, electrical, HVAC, remodeling, or a combination. It adapts content to whatever you do.

Can it post without my approval?

Never. Every post requires your explicit "go." You always see a preview first. Nothing goes live without your thumbs up.

How long does setup take?

2-3 business days. We connect your accounts, configure for your trade and service area, and do a 30-minute training call.

What if I need help?

Text or email us anytime. Premium includes a monthly strategy call. Most issues resolved within hours.

What happens if I cancel?

30-day notice. All your accounts stay yours. Credentials deleted within 7 days. No penalty. Setup fee is non-refundable.
