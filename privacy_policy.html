Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Trader Hawk AI assistant service ("Service"), operated by Steve Hawkins ("we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, and shares information when you use our AI-powered marketing and business assistant service.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Service to connect to your accounts, we access:

  • Social media accounts: Facebook Page name, ID, and access tokens; Instagram Business account ID and tokens — required to publish content on your behalf.
  • Google Workspace: Gmail access for sending/reading email, Google Calendar for event management, Google Docs for document creation, and Google Forms for survey generation — only when you explicitly authorize these integrations.
  • Content you create: Post text, images, flyers, documents, and scheduling preferences you provide through the Service.
  • Page insights: Basic engagement metrics (likes, comments, reach) to help optimize your content strategy.
  • iMessage communications: Messages you send to the Service's dedicated iMessage number are processed to execute your commands. We do not access your personal messages or conversations with other contacts.
  • Brand & style data: Sample posts, brand preferences, tone settings, and editing patterns used to train the style learning engine for your account.
  • Onboarding information: Name, phone number, email, brokerage, and market area provided during signup.

We do not collect personal messages (outside of your Service thread), friend lists, contact lists, or any data from users who interact with your Page or social accounts.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we access solely to:

  • Publish posts, photos, and videos to your Facebook Page and Instagram account as you direct.
  • Schedule future content at times you specify.
  • Generate AI-assisted content drafts, images, and flyers for your review and approval.
  • Send emails, manage calendar events, and create documents on your behalf via Google Workspace.
  • Create surveys and forms via Google Forms as you direct.
  • Train the style learning engine to match your brand voice and preferences.
  • Provide content performance summaries, marketing coaching, and strategy recommendations when requested.
  • Review contracts, inspection reports, and other documents you submit for analysis.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook/Instagram): When publishing content you have approved.
  • Google: When sending emails, managing calendar events, creating documents, or generating forms on your behalf.
  • AI service providers: Content text and document text may be sent to AI language models to generate drafts, summaries, and analysis. No personal data, access tokens, or credentials are shared with AI providers.
  • Payment processor (Stripe): Payment information is processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our systems.

4. Data Storage & Security

Access tokens and configuration are stored securely on private, encrypted hardware under our physical control. Each client's data is isolated — your credentials and content are never accessible to other clients. We do not store tokens in cloud databases or third-party services. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Retention

We retain your access tokens, account configuration, and style learning data only while you are an active subscriber of the Service. Upon cancellation or request, we will delete all stored tokens, credentials, style data, and cached content within 7 business days.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the Service's access via your Facebook Business Integrations settings.
  • Revoke Google access via your Google Account permissions.
  • Request deletion of all stored data by contacting us.
  • Request a copy of data we hold related to your account.
  • Cancel the Service at any time with 30 days' notice.

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below. We will confirm deletion within 7 business days. You can also remove the Service's access directly from Facebook Settings → Business Integrations and Google Account → Security → Third-party apps.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active users of material changes.
